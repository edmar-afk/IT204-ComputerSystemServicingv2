What is Network Media?

Network media is the actual path over which an electrical signal travels as it movrs from one component to another this information sheet describes the common types of network Media including twisted-pair cable, coaxial cable, fiber optic cable, and wireless.

Twisted Pair

Twisted pair cables is a type of cabling that is used for telephone communications and most modern ethernet networks.

Unshielded Twisted Pair

UTP Cable is a medium that is composed of wires UTP cable is used in a variety of networks. each of the eight individual copper wires in UTP cable is coverd by an insulating Material.

Shileded Twisted Pair

It is a cable combine the techniques of shielding cancellation, and wire twisting . Each pair of wires is wrapped in a metalic foil. the four pairs of wires then wrapped in an overall metallic braid foil.

Coaxial cable

It is a cable used in the transmission of video, communications, and audio. However, these cables are also used in netwroks and what allow a broadband cable internet connection using a cable modem

Plenum cable

is the cable that runs in plenun spaces of a building. construction, a plenun is separte space privided for air circulation for heating, ventalation, and air-conditioning(HVAC), typically in the space betwwen the structural ceiling and a drowp down ceiling.

Fiber Optic Cable

is cable thtat contains fibers(usually glas) coated in plastic that are used to send data by pulses of light

Wireless Communication

Wireless communication uses radio frequencies(RF) or infrared(IR) waves to transmit data between devices on LAN.for wireless LANs, a key component is the wireless hub, or access point used for signal distribution.

RA 10931

The Universal Access to Quality Tertiary Education Act is a law in the Philippines that institutionalises free tuition and exemption from other fees in state universities and colleges (SUCs), local universities and colleges (LUCs) in the Philippines. The law also foresees subsidies also for private higher education institutions. It is intended to give underprivileged Filipino students a chance to earn a college degree./p>
